This is a critical observation, and honestly, catching it now is a lifesaver. If you submit the app to Apple with the recording screen as the "front door" without authentication, it will almost certainly be rejected for two reasons:

Safety & Privacy: Apple is strict about user data. An app that records audio/text without a clear user association or "Owner" violates their privacy expectations.

App Completeness (Guideline 2.1): If the user can record but the data isn't "saved" to a profile because they aren't logged in, Apple views it as a broken or confusing user experience.
